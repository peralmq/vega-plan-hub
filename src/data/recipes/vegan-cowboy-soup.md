## Ingredients

| quantity | unit | key               | ingredient                 | notes                                |
| -------- | ---- | ----------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| 1        | tbsp | neutral-oil       | neutral oil                | for frying                           |
| 1        |      | onion             | yellow onion               | finely chopped                       |
| 2        |      | garlic            | garlic cloves              | minced                               |
| 300      | g    | vegan-mince       | vegan mince                | formable, or similar                 |
| 2        | tbsp | tomato-paste      | tomato paste               |                                      |
| 1        | tsp  | smoked-paprika    | smoked paprika             |                                      |
| 1        | tsp  | thyme             | dried thyme                |                                      |
| 0.5      | tsp  | ground-cumin      | ground cumin               | optional                             |
| 600      | g    | potato            | potatoes                   | peeled, diced                        |
| 400      | g    | tomato            | crushed tomatoes           |                                      |
| 500      | ml   | vegetable-stock   | vegetable stock            |                                      |
| 240      | g    | pinto-beans       | pinto beans                | drained and rinsed, or kidney beans  |
| 150      | g    | corn              | sweet corn                 | frozen or canned, drained            |
| 150      | g    | mixed-vegetables  | mixed vegetables           | optional, drained                    |
| 200      | ml   | oat-cream         | oat cooking cream          | or soy cooking cream                 |
| 1        | tbsp | soy-sauce         | soy sauce                  | optional, for depth                  |
|          |      | salt              | salt                       | to taste                             |
|          |      | black-pepper      | black pepper               | to taste                             |
|          |      | jalapeno          | jalapeno                   | optional garnish for adults          |

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a large pot. Saute onion and garlic until soft. 🍳
2. Add vegan mince and cook until lightly browned.
3. Stir in tomato paste, smoked paprika, thyme, and cumin. Cook 1 minute.
4. Add potatoes, crushed tomatoes, vegetable stock, beans, corn, and mixed vegetables. Bring to a boil.
5. Reduce heat and simmer 12 to 15 minutes, until the potatoes are tender.
6. Stir in oat cream and soy sauce. Season with salt and black pepper.
7. Serve hot. Add jalapeno at the table if desired. 🥣✨

## Notes

- Mild and kid-friendly. Add extra heat at the table for adults. 👶✨
- For more smokiness, add an extra pinch of smoked paprika.
