## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 225°C (437°F). Spread half a pack of frozen Anammas Formbara Färs on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Season with salt and pepper, drizzle with oil, and mix well. Roast in the oven for about 15 minutes until browned and firm for better texture.
2. Peel and slice the red onion and garlic. Peel and dice the carrot. Finely chop the dried chipotle chilies (remove seeds for less heat). Chop the chocolate and set aside.
3. Heat a large pot with plenty of oil. Sauté onion, carrot, chipotle, and garlic until the onion softens.
4. Add oregano, smoked paprika, and cumin. Fry for a minute until fragrant.
5. Add BBQ sauce, crushed tomatoes, red wine, water, both soy sauces, and vegetable fond. Stir, then add the roasted mince. Bring to a boil, then simmer on low heat with a lid for 30–60 minutes (the longer, the better).
6. After 30–40 minutes, taste and adjust seasoning with more soy sauce or spices if needed. With about 15 minutes left, add the chocolate, beans, and corn. Let simmer so the flavors meld. Add water if needed.
7. Finish by tasting for salt and squeezing in the lime juice.
8. Serve with rice, nachos, guacamole, vegan sour cream, and salad. Enjoy! ✨
