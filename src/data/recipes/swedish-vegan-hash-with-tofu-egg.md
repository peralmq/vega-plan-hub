## Ingredients

| quantity | unit   | key               | ingredient                | notes            |
| -------- | ------ | ----------------- | ------------------------- | ---------------- |
| 1        | bag    | vegan-hash        | vegan hash (pyttipanna)   | Felix or similar |
| 400      | g      | tofu              | firm tofu                 |                  |
| 1        | tbsp   | margarine         | margarine or vegan butter |                  |
| 1        | tsp    | kala-namak        | kala namak (black salt)   |                  |
| 2        | tbsp   | nutritional-yeast | nutritional yeast (nooch) |                  |
| 0.5      | tsp    | salt              | salt                      |                  |
| 1        | jar    | pickled-beetroot  | pickled beetroot          |                  |
| 1        | bottle | ketchup           | ketchup                   |                  |
| 1        | bottle | hp-sauce          | HP sauce                  |                  |

## Instructions

1. Fry the hash: Heat a large skillet and fry the vegan hash according to package instructions, about 13 minutes until golden and heated through. 🥔
2. Prepare the "eggy" tofu: Press the tofu for at least 10 minutes to remove excess water. Cut into cubes or crumble.
3. Heat margarine in a skillet. Fry the tofu over medium heat until it gets some color.
4. Sprinkle over kala namak, nutritional yeast, and salt. Stir so everything is mixed and the tofu gets an "eggy" flavor and aroma. Fry for another minute. 🍳
5. Serve the hash with "eggy" tofu, pickled beetroot, ketchup, and HP sauce. Enjoy! ✨

## Notes

- Kala namak gives the tofu an authentic egg flavor—available in specialty stores or online.
- Nutritional yeast (nooch) adds umami and a "cheesy" taste.
- Top with fresh herbs or black pepper if desired.
- Perfect as a quick weekday meal or brunch!
